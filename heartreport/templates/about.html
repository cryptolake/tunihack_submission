What is Heart Report?

Heart report is an app to support doctors and people who are concerned about their cardiovascular health, by training a model through thousands of medical records of heart problems, we can offer you insight into your heart's health!

Why Heart disease exactly?

Arguably the heart is the most important part of us, but in recent times heart disease rates continued climbing and climbing due to multitude, HeartReport aims to show the importance of healthy habits and how to detect the signs of heart problems and also help doctors in supporting their diagnosis.

Can heart report replace doctors?

NO, heart report is supported by statistical modeling and doesn't offer the insights of a doctor, it's made so it can help people be more aware of the dangers of heart disease and help doctors in their diagnosis.
